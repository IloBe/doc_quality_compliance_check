# Standard Operating Procedure: Risk Management Procedure

**Document ID:** SSP-SOP-RISK-001<br>
**Version:** 1.1<br>
**Status:** Draft<br>
**Owner:** Risk Manager / Quality Lead<br>

---

## 1. Purpose

Define the procedure for identifying, evaluating, treating, and monitoring product risks.

---

## 2. Scope

Covers software, AI, privacy, security, supplier, and operational risks across product lifecycle phases.

---

## 3. Risk Method

**Risk Score = Probability x Impact**

| Probability (1-5) | Impact (1-5) | Risk Score |
|-------------------|--------------|------------|
| Defined by matrix | Defined by matrix | 1-25 |

---

## 4. Risk Process

1. Identify risks.
2. Analyze probability and impact.
3. Evaluate against acceptance criteria.
4. Define treatment plan and owner.
5. Track residual risk and closure evidence.
6. Reassess at planned intervals.

---

---

## 6. Treatment Strategies

- Avoid
- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Accept

---

## 7. Review Cadence

| Review Type | Frequency | Owner |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| Full review | Monthly | Risk Manager |
| Triggered review | Incident / major change | Risk and cross-functional team |

---

## 8. Governance and Escalation

- High/critical risks require escalation to management.
- Accepted residual risks require documented rationale and approval.

---

## 9. Inputs / Outputs

**Inputs:** incidents, findings, change requests, supplier issues, security findings.  
**Outputs:** updated register, treatment plans, residual risk decisions, governance reports.
