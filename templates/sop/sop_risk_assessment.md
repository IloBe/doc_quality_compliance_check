## 1. Risk Assessment Methodology

**Risk Score = Probability × Impact**

| Probability | Score | Impact | Score |
|------------|-------|--------|-------|
| Very Low (<5%) | 1 | Negligible | 1 |
| Low (5-25%) | 2 | Minor | 2 |
| Medium (25-50%) | 3 | Moderate | 3 |
| High (50-75%) | 4 | Major | 4 |
| Very High (>75%) | 5 | Critical | 5 |

**Risk Level:**
- 1–4: Low (Green) — Monitor
- 5–9: Medium (Yellow) — Mitigate
- 10–15: High (Orange) — Mitigate urgently
- 16–25: Critical (Red) — Escalate immediately
